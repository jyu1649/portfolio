About Me

I’m a software engineer who enjoys building reliable, scalable systems that solve real problems. With over 5+ years worth of professional experience, I have designed and maintained APIs, worked with service-oriented architectures, and focused on making systems observable, testable, and scalable.

I value code quality, maintainability, strong logging and monitoring, and testing at the right levels so services can be deployed with confidence. I’ve worked in environments where services are built, tested, and released independently, and I enjoy collaborating closely with engineers, product managers, and stakeholders to ship features incrementally and responsibly.

I’m a curious learner who enjoys picking up new tools and technologies when the problem calls for it. Whether it’s learning a new framework, improving performance in an existing system, or debugging a tricky production issue, I like digging in, understanding the root cause, and leaving the system better than I found it. I’m most excited by teams that value collaboration, ownership, continuous improvement, and healthy work-life balance.
